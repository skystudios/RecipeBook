Paani Puri Recipe

(Serves 2)

Ingredients

  • Mint - 3 bundles
  • Coriander - 1 bundle
  • Green Chillies - 6
  • Ginger - 1.5 inch
  • Black Salt - 21 grams
  • Pani Puri Masala - 12 grams
  • Chaat Masala - 5 grams
  • Imli Chutney - 450 grams
  • Water (for grinding) - 610 grams
  • Water (for pani) - 1350 grams
  • Lemon - 1 whole

Instructions

  1. Clean and sort mint.
  2. Clean and sort coriander.
  3. Chop mint and coriander together.
  4. Grind in the big mixer jar in 3 batches along with ice, green chillies, and ginger.
  5. Grind in small jar in multiple batches.
  6. Strain and throw away the solids.
  7. Add water, imli chutney, pani puri masala, chaat masala, black salt, and lemon juice and mix well.
  8. Have the pani puri the next day.
