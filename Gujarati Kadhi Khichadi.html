Gujarati Kadhi Khichadi



Khichadi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice - 80 grams
  • Green moong dal - 80 grams
  • Ghee - 1 TBSP
  • Cumin Seeds - 1/2 TSP
  • Hing - 1/2 TSP
  • Water - 850 grams
  • Salt to taste
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/4 TSP

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and dal together until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak for 20-25 mins.
    • Meanwhile, get the kadhi ready.
  3. Once soaked, discard the water.
  4. Set pressure cooker on high heat & add ghee, cumin seeds, and Hing. Let the cumin seeds crackle.
  5. Add soaked rice & dal.
  6. Stir well & cook for 1-2 mins.
  7. Add water, salt, and turmeric powder.
  8. Stir well and bring to boil.
  9. Remove the froth 2-3 times.
  10. Place the lid and pressure cook for 3 whistles on medium high flame.
  11. Let the pressure cooker de-pressurize naturally.
  12. Khichadi is ready.

Kadhi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Curd - 135 grams
  • Besan - 15 grams
  • Ginger chilli paste - 1 inch ginger & 1 green chilli
  • Salt to taste
  • Water - 360 grams
  • Cumin Seeds - 1/4 TSP
  • Mustard Seeds - 1/4 TSP
  • Whole Spices Mix
    • Cinnamon - 0.5 inch
    • Cloves - 4 Nos.
    • Red Chillies - 2 Nos.
    • Hing - 1/4 TSP
    • Curry Leaves - 6
    • Methi Seeds - 1/8 TSP
  • Sugar - 35 grams

Instructions

  1. Get a bowl.
  2. Add curd, besan, ginger chilli paste & salt.
  3. Whisk it to make a smooth paste.
  4. Add water & whisk to make a liquidy batter.
  5. Heat ghee in a kadhai.
  6. Add cumin seeds & mustard seeds and let crackle.
  7. Add the whole spices mix.
  8. Once spices crackle, pour the batter and bring it to boil.
  9. Add salt & sugar.
  10. Let it boil until desired consistency is obtained.
  11. Kadhi is ready.
