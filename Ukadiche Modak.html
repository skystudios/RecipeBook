Ukadiche Modak

Yield: 8 pieces

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
    • Rice Flour - 100 grams
    • Water - 225 grams
    • Ghee - 1/4 TSP
    • Salt - 1/8 TSP
  • For the stuffing:
    • Grated Coconut - 67 grams
    • Jaggery - 134 grams
    • Cardamom Powder - 1 TSP
    • Nutmeg - 1/4 TSP
    • Khuskhus - 1/2 TSP
    • Ghee - 1/2 TSP
    • Rice Flour - 1 TBSP (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and keep the heat to low.
  2. Add khus khus and fry until seeds start crackling.
  3. Add coconut. Jaggery, cardamom powder and nutmeg.
  4. Mix well and cook on low heat.
  5. Cook for 7-9 minutes. Donâ€™t overcook as the jaggery will harden later.
  6. Keep the mixture aside to let it cool down.
  7. Now prepare the dough.
  8. In a pan, add water, ghee, and salt.
  9. Turn on the heat and bring it to boil.
  10. Reduce the heat and add the rice flour gradually. Stirring the mixing the flour the whole time.
  11. Switch off the flame and remove the pan from the stove top and cover for 4-5 minutes.
  12. Now take the dough in a large plate or thali and knead.
  13. The dough should be smooth and pliable (not too hard).
  14. Make small balls and keep it aside.
  15. Flatten the dough balls and start making modaks.
  16. When all the modaks are shaped, keep them aside and start preparing for steaming.
  17. Take an idli steamer and pour some water in it.
  18. When the water starts to boil, put modaks on a greased pan and cover the steamer with the lid.
  19. Steam for 13 minutes on medium-low heat.
  20. Your modaks are ready.
