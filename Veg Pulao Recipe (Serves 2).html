Veg Pulao Recipe

(Serves 2)

Ingredients

  • Rice - 1 cup
  • Water - 2 cups
  • Salt - To taste
  • Ghee - 2 TBSP
  • Onion - 1 medium-sized (thinly sliced)
  • Green Chillies - 2
  • Mix Vegetables - 1 cup
    • Potato
    • Beans
    • Carrots
    • Green Peas
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 1 TSP
  • Masala Mix 1
    • Bay leaf - 1
    • Star Anise - 1
    • Mace - 1 long strand
    • Cumin - 1 TSP
    • Green Cardamom - 3
    • Cloves - 4
    • Cinnamon - 1 inch

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice until water runs clear.
  2. Soak for 15 mins and drain.
  3. Heat ghee in a cooker.
  4. Add masala mix 1 & sauté.
  5. Add ginger garlic paste & sauté.
  6. Add veggies & sauté for 2-3 mins.
  7. Add water & salt.
  8. Bring to boil.
  9. Add rice & stir.
  10. Cover the cooker & cook for 1 whistle (medium flame).
  11. Let the cooker depressurize naturally.
  12. Veg pulao is ready.

Boondi Raita Recipe

Ingredients

  • Curd - 250 grams
  • Sugar - 40 grams
  • Water - 100 grams
  • Salt - To taste
  • Black Salt - To taste
  • Cumin Powder - 1/4 TSP
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1/4 TSP
  • Boondi - As per preference (soaked in warm water for 5 minutes)

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients well.
  2. Add soaked boondi.
  3. Test from VSCode
