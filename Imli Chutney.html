Imli Chutney

(Enough for 4-5 months or 2 times pani puri)

Ingredients

  • Imli - 60 grams
  • Dates (seedless) - 125 grams
  • Jaggery - 375 grams
  • Salt - To taste
  • Masala Mix 1
    • Black Salt - 1/2 TSP
    • Ginger Powder - 1/4 TSP
    • Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder - 1/2 TBSP
    • Jeera Powder - 1/2 TBSP
    • Black Pepper - A pinch
  • Water - 500 grams

Instructions

  1. Soak the imli and khajoor separately in hot water for 20 mins.
  2. Squeeze the imli to remove the pulp.
  3. Mix imli pulp and khajoor in grinder and make fine paste.
  4. Strain the paste in a kadhai and remove fibers.
  5. Add jaggery and Masala Mix 1.
  6. Turn on the flame and let the jaggery melt.
  7. Remove the scum floating on top.
  8. Cook the chutney for 30 mins.
  9. Turn off the flame.
  10. Chutney is ready.
